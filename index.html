About

Open World Craft Game is Open World and survival, the first concept was released in 14 Jan 2018 (official). It’s about survival, throughout warfares and peaces. It’s said to be set in 2600s or later. The main character is Future Sang, The purpose of this franchise is understanding about future livings, situations, backgrounds, etc…. This series’s story is never end, keep moving forward.

Gameplay

Story mode gamemode (Gameplay: ~2 hours)

  • Character: Future Sang
  • Stats: HP, Stamina
  • HP: if he dies, you will be sent to previous checkpoint
  • Stamina: for swimming, running, flying, dodge
  • Swimming: if he still swim in the sea, his leg will stop working and be drowned.
  • Running: if you force him to run, he will have heartbreaking (which also happens in rl) his hp will be decreased gradually till death.
  • Flying: if he still fly in the midair while running out of stamina, he will be falling and dies.
  • Dodge: if Stamina still be here, he will be able to dodge.
  • Holdable items:
    • Standard: Fire punch
    • Flame katana (Act 3)
    • Golden staff (Act 3)
  • Boss fight functions:
    • Act 1: If you do not defeat Geo in 5 minutes, airplane will send nuke, will end up the same in story as result
    • Act 2: If you do not defeat Sangerus.exe in 5 minutes, Sangerus.exe will regain his hp for 25% and summon wave of minor of Sangerus.exe come to kill the player
    • Act 3: If you do not kill Dragon in 5 minutes, Black mountain island will create some eruptions if lava hit you, you might die.
  • Boss fight unique:
    • If player dodged perfectly, you can do quick event to hit them with more hp.
  • Attack types:
    • Light attack
    • Heavy attack
    • Special attack
      • Fire punch: throws a fireball
      • Flame katana / Golden staff: throws flame katana like boomerang
    • Ultimate attack
      • Fire punch: slams to the ground and exploded
      • Flame katana: slice the enemy 10 times with movement speed like light
      • Golden staff: Makes the staff twice bigger then will shrink 30 secs gradually
